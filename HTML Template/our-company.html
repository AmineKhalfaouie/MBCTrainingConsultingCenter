What is MBC Training

MBC Training Center & Consulting is deeply committed to empowering individuals and fostering opportunities for career and technology training. Our focus spans various communities, including the homeless, low-income families, victims of sex trafficking, senior citizens, ex-felons, and veterans. We believe that everyone deserves a chance at a fulfilling career and the means to develop their potential, regardless of their background or circumstances. We have built strong relationships with these individuals to jumpstart their road to success. Moreover, MBC is proud to have cultivated strong relationships with esteemed trainers throughout Indiana. These partnerships enable us to offer both long-term and short-term opportunities tailored to meet the unique needs of our students and residents in the area. We partner with various industries to ensure our training programs are aligned with current demands.
